Course Description

DHC-8-400 Type Training is an interactive state-of-the-art web based training and is designed to develop skills, knowledge and performance among maintenance and fl ight crew personnel. The course is designed to AICC and SCORM standards and combines rich multimedia graphics with limited use of text, audio narration and a high degree of interactive elements.
